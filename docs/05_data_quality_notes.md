# Data Quality Notes

## Issue 1

Revenue data was available only for 2022.

### Impact

Revenue trends across multiple years could not be analysed.

### Resolution

The Revenue & Customer dashboard was limited to 2022 only.

---

## Issue 2

Fuel purchase data covered 2022–2025 while revenue data covered only 2022.

### Impact

Revenue and fuel metrics were not directly compared over time.

### Resolution

Separate dashboards were created.

---

## Issue 3

Maintenance records contained only January 2022.

### Impact

Maintenance trends were not meaningful.

### Resolution

The dashboard focuses on maintenance cost and downtime by truck rather than monthly trends.

---

## Issue 4

Driver Monthly Metrics contained only two driver IDs.

### Impact

The table was not representative of the entire driver population.

### Resolution

Driver analysis relied primarily on the Drivers, Trips, Fuel Purchases and Safety Incidents tables.
